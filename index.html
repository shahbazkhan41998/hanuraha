About Hanuruha

Hanuruha is a premium specialized, women-focused rehabilitation centre committed to holistic recovery, emotional healing, and long-term well-being. With the legacy of treating 1400+ women over 9 years through Shree Kendra, Hanuruha brings specialised care with modern facilities, experienced professionals, and a healing environment designed for dignity and recovery.

Every woman deserves a second chance, a safe space, and compassionate support. At Hanuruha, healing is not just treatment—it is rebuilding values, restoring identity, and restarting life with confidence.
